Dave Dawson is a designer working with organizations making a positive impact.

In the last seven years, he has designed websites and products for Partners In Health, The Human Rights Campaign, Surfrider Foundation, Four Seasons, FDR Four Freedoms Park, #TackleEbola, Maker's Row, Avicii, Film Society at Lincoln Center, Gary Hustwit, The Feast, and littleBits.

Before starting his own design studio, Cottleston Pie, Dave worked as the Principal Designer at Blue State Digital in New York City.
