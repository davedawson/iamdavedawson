What I do

Product Design

My favorite product teams are the small and scrappy ones. I value moving quickly, prototyping, testing, and iterating. But, the most important part of product design is understanding and defining the problem we're solving. The earlier we do that, and validate our assumptions with users, the smoother the entire process will be.

Pixel perfect designs are a good goal to have, but they're not something that should get in the way of a proof of concept. I believe in getting something out quickly, testing it with real users, and iterating our way to pixel perfection. I’m a designer who codes, so I'm able to jump in with your team to prototype ideas, and even style base components to get to launch quickly.

Where I can help

  • UX research
  • User flow mapping & wireframes
  • UI design
  • Prototyping & testing

Website Design

Designing a website is designing for message and action. And of course, aligning stakeholders, defining content requirements and processes, outlining a website’s architecture, visualizing a brand’s identity in a digital medium, and making sure it looks great and performs well on all devices.

I’ve designed scores of websites over the past 15 years. Many of those have been cause-based, but I’ve also worked in the arts and editorial spaces. I believe that design is a tool to serve a website’s content and should be only as visible as it needs to be. I design websites that support the message and the priorities of your organization.

Where I can help

  • Project road mapping
  • Kickoff meetings
  • Information architecture
  • Wireframing
  • Visual design
  • Front-end development

Creative Direction & Consulting

I work with creative teams to produce content across mediums with a consistent message and visual style. As a creative director, I establish and execute a creative vision, lead teams, and establish a creative culture.

As a consultant I work with organizations looking for an outside perspective on their digital presence and strategy. I’ll work with you to audit your current digital strategy and create a roadmap for improvements.

Select projects

  • Tom Steyer 2020 In 2019, I took a sabbatical from my design studio to serve as the creative director on the Tom Steyer presidential campaign. I led a team of 35 creatives who were responsible for anything visual in both digital, print, and some TV.
