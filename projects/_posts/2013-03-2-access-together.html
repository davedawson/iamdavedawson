Access Together is the brainchild of John Schimmel, a developer who focuses on accessible technology. We've been working together over the past year on a mobile web app in addition to a desktop site.

The goal of Access Together right now is to gather reliable accessibility data about venues. Users are able to search the database for locations that meet their specific accessibility requirements.

The app was recently added to Foursquare's app list, making it even easier to accessify your community when you check in to Foursquare. Get on it!
