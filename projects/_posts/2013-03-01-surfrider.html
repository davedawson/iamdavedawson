Surfrider Foundation approached Blue State Digital about a redesign in 2010.

Working closely with Wolasi Konu, we redesigned surfrider.org for action.

In a fun and collaborative process with the client, we were able to land on a design that presents an appropriate first impression of the organization and their goals. Let's keep these beaches clean so we can get out there and enjoy them!
