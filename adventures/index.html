Always on an adventure

I

On a Wednesday

A project of pure curiosity. For years, every Wednesday, Bekka Palmer and I went out into New York City to ask people what they were up to. We recorded short responses and a photo. The product was a quick look into what everyday people are doing around town.

Money Over People

In collaboration with Nick Beattie, we created a website that lists out how much money a politician has accepted from the NRA during their career.

Get Biked

I get attached to my bikes, and whenever it's time to move to another bike I want to make sure that it goes to a good home. So I created an online community in NYC where people could find share their bikes for sale and ensure that it went to someone who would take care of it.

The Works

I owned and operated a small, shared workspace in Brooklyn for two years. We had around a dozen desks in the space, made lots of new friends, held community events, and a few great parties.

SongFight

I teamed up with Jonny Gotham and Bekka Palmer to turn this Twitter conversation into an game where you can fight your friends with music.
