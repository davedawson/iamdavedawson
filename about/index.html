About me

Hi! I'm Dave and I live in California with my wife, Mary, and our dog, Spock.

This is where I'm supposed to give myself some sort of professional label. But this is exactly where I struggle to do so. Those labels are contextual and constantly changing. I've been called a husband, a studio owner, a creative director, a cyclist, a designer, an artist, a road-tripper, a front-end developer, and a whole lot more.

The through line of most of those is that I'm happiest when I'm meeting new people and helping others.

Work

In 2012, I started a tiny digital studio called [**Cottleston Pie**.](http://www.havesomecottlestonpie.com/) We create websites and products for progressive causes, organizations, and companies. Over the past 8 years we've been incredibly fortunate to work with some truly inspiring people and causes. I'm grateful that I get to do the work that I do.

Before starting Cottleston Pie, I was the principal designer at [Blue State](http://bluestate.co/) in Manhattan. And in 2019 I took a sabbatical from my design studio to serve as the creative director on the [Tom Steyer presidential campaign](https://www.nytimes.com/2020/03/08/opinion/tom-steyer-president.html). While the campaign didn't end the way we'd hoped, I'm incredibly proud of my team, the work we made, and of course Tom.

I've been privileged to work in the progressive space for most of my career, and have been able to create work for dozens of wonderful organizations. A few career highlights are [Partners in Health](http://pih.org), [Human Rights Campaign](http://hrc.org), [Harper's Magazine](http://harpers.org), [Surfrider](http://surfrider.org), and [Film at Lincoln Center](http://filmlinc.org).

We're actively looking for new projects, so please [reach out](http://www.havesomecottlestonpie.com/hire/).

Personal

I made a promise to myself to never become complacent with work or life. And that has been one of my most important guiding principles. Every day, I try appreciate what's in front of me, and to push myself to always be learning something new.

In 2017 my wife, our dog, Spock, and I put all of our stuff in storage and drove out of New York to explore the U.S. Originally scheduled as a six-month road trip, we ended up traveling for two-years. Some of the highlights are on Instagram with the #dawsonsontherun hashtag.

We recently settled into Encinitas, CA, where we're planning to stay put for a while.

Office Hours

Each week, I set aside an hour for open office hours to chat with people new to the design industry.

There’s a lot I’m working on improving about myself and my business. But, I’ve made a few things, worked with some people, started a few companies, and I’ve survived! There are so many people that have shared their experiences with me along the way, and it’s a privilege to be able to share my experiences with others.
